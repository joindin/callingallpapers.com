Call for Papers

Health in Justice Conference 2016 - Equality in practice: Managing long term conditions in secure environments. Closes in 0 minutes

Authors are invited to submit abstracts for consideration to appear at the Health in Justice Conference 2016.

Two presentation formats are invited:

Poster presentations
Written presentations of research. Presentations will allow for discussion between the author and interested parties in an informal setting. Posters should be easily readable from a distance of one metre, and should be prepared to be presented as an A1 poster. Hand-outs are encouraged and presenters are responsible for producing their own poster.

Workshop sessions
Interactive sessions designed to improve skills and knowledge among those working in the health in justice system. Subject matter is open, but should be designed to address specific, topical issues. Workshops will be 40 minutes long, ideally guided by a PowerPoint Presentation and hand-outs are encouraged. Workshops should be topical, interesting, informative and enjoyable. Workshop submissions may include interactive workshops, experiential presentations and programme descriptions.

This call is open. You can find information on how to apply here.
